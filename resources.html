Resources for Growing Young Minds

Explore these resources to deepen your understanding of sustainable agriculture, healthy eating habits, and the wonders of the natural world. We've compiled a list of books, websites, and videos suitable for children of all ages, and for curious adults as well!

Recommended Books:

  • The Secret Garden by Frances Hodgson Burnett (Classic children's story about the magic of nature and gardening)
  • How a Seed Grows by Helene J. Jordan (VIDEO) (Simple and engaging explanation of the plant life cycle)
  • We Are Water Protectors by Carole Lindstrom (VIDEO) (Beautiful story about protecting water resources and the environment)

Educational Websites:

  • [KidsGardening.org] (Website with interactive activities, gardening tips, and kid-friendly recipes)
  • [Natural Resources Conservation Service: Soil for Kids] (Fun and informative website about soil science and its importance)
  • [Nutrition Activities and Games] (Engaging website with games, and activities about healthy eating)

Inspiring Videos:

  • "The Pollinators" by National Geographic [YouTube]" (Documentary exploring the importance of pollinators and their role in food production)
  • "How to Grow Your Own Herbs" by Gardening Know How [YouTube]" (Simple and practical video guide on growing herbs at home)
  • "Composting 101" by Pure Living for Life [YouTube]" (Informative video explaining the benefits and process of composting)
